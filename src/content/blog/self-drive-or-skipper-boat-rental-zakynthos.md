When booking a boat rental in Zakynthos, the first real decision you face is this: do you want to drive the boat yourself or hire a professional skipper to do it for you? Both options get you on the water and take you to the same stunning destinations. The difference is in the experience, the level of responsibility, and what you want to actually feel during your time at sea.

## What Is a Self-Drive Boat Rental

A self-drive rental means you are the one operating the boat. No skipper, no guide. You set off from the port after a short safety briefing and navigate the coastline at your own pace.

### What makes it a good choice

- You decide where to go and when to stop
- You set your own pace without waiting for others
- It is the more affordable option of the two
- The freedom of having the boat entirely to yourself

### What to keep in mind

- You are responsible for the boat while it is in your care
- You need to stay within the areas covered in your briefing
- Fuel costs are on top of the rental fee
- It requires basic confidence with a simple motorised vehicle

## What Is a Skipper Boat Rental

A skipper rental means a professional captain comes with the boat. You show up, get on board, and the skipper handles everything: navigation, anchoring, route decisions, and any technical aspects of the trip.

### What makes it a good choice

- Zero responsibility for navigation or boat handling
- The skipper knows the waters, the best swimming spots, and the hidden coves
- Ideal for larger groups where one person should not be responsible for everyone
- You can drink, relax, and fully enjoy the experience without any obligations
- Better suited for rough sea days where less experienced drivers might struggle

### What to keep in mind

- It costs more than a self-drive rental
- The route is guided, so you have less spontaneous control over stops
- You are sharing the experience with a third person on board

## Side by Side: Key Differences

### Experience level needed

Self-drive requires no sailing experience but does expect you to be comfortable handling a small motorboat after a briefing. The skipper option requires nothing from you except showing up.

### Cost

Self-drive is the more budget-friendly option. The skipper rental costs more because you are paying for a professional's time and expertise on top of the boat itself.

### Control over the route

Self-drive gives you full autonomy. You can linger at a beach as long as you want, skip a stop entirely, or change direction. With a skipper, the route is more structured, though a good skipper will always be flexible about swimming stops and timing.

### Group size and dynamics

For couples or small groups of friends who want an independent adventure, self-drive works very well. For larger groups, families with young children, or anyone who wants to focus entirely on relaxing and not managing logistics, the skipper option is the smarter choice.

## Which One Should You Book

There is no universally better option. It comes down to what kind of day you want.

Choose self-drive if:

- You want maximum freedom and independence
- You are comfortable taking on light responsibility
- You are travelling with a small group who all want the same experience
- Budget is a factor

Choose a skipper if:

- You want to fully switch off and be taken care of
- Your group is large or includes children
- You want local knowledge of the best spots
- You are celebrating something special and want the day to feel effortless
